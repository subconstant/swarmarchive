swarm archive

swarm axes is a decentralized short film / video art festival organized in synchronized independent viewing-swarm events. this page serves as an online viewing room and archive of some of the works distributed in the festival. you can read more about the swarm axes irl events here.

developed collectively by solo show online and arcana.

the swarm archive is open every month around a full moon.
